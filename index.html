About 933 Proxie

Network Architecture

Residential proxies utilize IP addresses assigned by ISPs to real residential devices, routing traffic through legitimate user connections for high authenticity and low detection rates.

Datacenter proxies are hosted in high-performance server facilities, offering superior speed and stability but potentially easier to identify by advanced systems.

Mobile proxies leverage 4G/5G carrier networks, providing dynamic IPs that mimic mobile user behavior, ideal for bypassing strict anti-bot measures.

Rotation strategies include per-request changes or sticky sessions (1-60 minutes), with pool sizes scaled to demand for reliable TTL.

Protocols & Authentication

Supports HTTP/HTTPS and SOCKS5 protocols. SOCKS5 enables UDP traffic and enhanced authentication.

Authentication options: username:password or IP whitelisting (up to 5 IPs per slot). Sticky sessions maintain connections.

Example connection: http://username:password@proxy.933proxie.com:8080

Geo Coverage & Limitations

Coverage spans 150+ countries with city/ISP targeting available upon request, subject to inventory.

Stockouts are managed via regional fallbacks, waitlists, or partial refunds. Availability may vary by demand; rare locations could have wait times.

Performance Expectations

Latency: 50-150ms (datacenter), 100-400ms (residential/mobile) depending on region.

Throughput: Up to 200Mbps (datacenter), 20-70Mbps (residential), 10-50Mbps (mobile).

Concurrency per slot: Varies by product; exceeding limits may activate rate throttling to maintain quality.
